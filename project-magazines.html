MAGAZINES

ELLE

ELLE is a global fashion and lifestyle magazine. I design media kits and marketing materials for the brand. Charts and graphics present information with clarity and visual balance. Data becomes part of the editorial language.

ELLE Girl

ELLEgirl was a fashion and beauty magazine for older teens, a younger take on the ELLE spirit with style and trend at its heart. As art director, I led a full redesign of the magazine, shaping layouts, concepts, and visual flow from start to print. The new look helped redefine the brand and was awarded Magazine of the Year.

Cosmopolitan

Cosmopolitan is one of the most widely read magazines in the world. I work as senior designer on the national edition and lead fashion and beauty photoshoots. I shaped editorial layouts while guiding the visual direction of shoots, keeping the look strong, current, and unmistakably Cosmo.

Tech Magazines

Technology magazines focused on complex ideas and detailed information. I design layouts and icons that bring order and clarity to technical content. An industrial visual language shapes the pages and supports understanding. Design stays functional, structured, and direct.
