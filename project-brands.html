BRANDS

Testarossa Winery

Testarossa Winery is a California winery known for handcrafted wines and intimate tasting experiences. I worked across print, digital, and events to shape a cohesive visual world for the brand — from wine labels and packaging to large-scale graphics and immersive event spaces. My focus was on clear design, strong typography, and smooth guest journeys, creating visuals that feel warm, intentional, and true to the winery’s story.

Passion Embrace

Passion Embrace is a beauty brand inspired by tango — bold lipstick, close movement, shared emotion. I shaped the digital experience with rhythm and flow, turning the brand’s energy into a smooth, intuitive design. In print and packaging, I focused on mood, texture, and restraint, keeping every detail elegant and connected. For video, I guided the visual story — capturing motion, touch, and color as one.

Pivot Point Dance Company

Pivot Point Dance Company is a contemporary dance company exploring movement, presence, and connection. I work inside the company as both dancer and designer, creating the visual language for live performances. I design merch and show identities — posters, tickets, social content, and reels — using contrast, rhythm, and color to echo the choreography. This is where movement becomes graphic, and design moves with the body.

@happytowasteless

@happytowasteless is a local, woman-owned brand sharing skills through masterclasses and workshops. I designed brochures that feel light and intentional, with black-and-white graphics giving space to the content. Every page carries a calm, thoughtful touch, letting the workshops speak for themselves.

Springer Elementary School

Springer Elementary School is a lively place where learning is fun and full of energy. I refresh all the event merch — t-shirts, keychains, and souvenirs — bringing funny bees, playful kids’ illustrations, and school icons to life. Each year, I lead the walkathon designs, making the event colorful, cheerful, and full of smiles for students, families, and teachers.
