PRINTS

Wine Labels

Wine labels focused on what matters inside the bottle. I use minimal visual elements to highlight information, clarity, and paper texture. Every detail serves a purpose. Nothing else lives on the label except what matters — means nothing else in the bottle except the natural wine and its pure taste.

California Postcards

California Postcards. I draw places in California that feel personal and memorable. I use ink and bold, bright colors for the backgrounds. Each postcard captures moments from my life around these places.

Odesa Postcards

Odesa Postcards. I capture Odesa from the inside — small pieces of daily life and hidden corners. I use ink and white pen on craft-colored paper, like pages from a notebook. The postcards feel personal, intimate, and lived-in.

Italy Postcards

Italy Postcards. I document Italy during travel, noticing the rhythm between crowded tourist spots and quiet corners of small towns. I use ink pen and color markers on textured white paper. Each postcard records a fleeting moment and the contrast of public and private spaces.

Zoomer Activity Box

Kids Activity Box. The box contains materials, guides, and indoor quest activities to explore one artist. I connect each activity to the artist through their work, style, and portrait. The design makes learning hands-on, visual, and playful.

Business Cards

My personal business cards. Each card is unique, made from my drawings — no two look the same. I use a soft grey for the front, black and white for essentials, and bright toxic yellow on the back. The yellow acts as a spark — a sign of creativity, energy, and a playful touch that surprises. Means nothing - just The yellow is just a little surprise.
